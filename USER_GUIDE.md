# Karga Connect User Guide

This guide explains how to use Karga Connect in plain English.

## 1. What Karga Connect is

Karga Connect is a trucking marketplace where:
- Shippers post cargo they need delivered.
- Truckers post available trucks and routes.
- Both sides negotiate through bids and in-app chat.
- Contracts are generated in-app.
- Active shipments are tracked in real time.

You can also join the Broker Program to earn referral commissions.

## 2. Account types

Karga has 4 user types:
- Guest: Can browse listings and preview the app.
- Shipper: Posts cargo and books truckers.
- Trucker: Posts trucks and bids on cargo.
- Admin: Reviews platform fee payments and manages system operations.

You can switch between Shipper and Trucker from your profile after sign-in.

## 3. Getting started

### Step 1: Open the app
- Open the Karga app in your browser.
- You can browse as a guest immediately.

### Step 2: Sign in with phone OTP
- Enter your PH phone number.
- Get a 6-digit OTP.
- Enter OTP to verify.

### Step 3: Complete your profile (first time only)
- Name (required)
- Business name (optional)
- Email (optional)
- Referral code (optional)
- Choose role: Shipper or Trucker

After this, your account is ready.

## 5. Guest mode

As a guest, you can:
- Browse cargo and truck listings
- Use search and filters
- View marketplace previews

As a guest, you cannot:
- Post listings
- Place bids
- Open private chats
- View contracts
- Access profile data

If you try a protected action, the app opens sign-in automatically.

## 6. Home screen basics

On Home, you can:
- Switch between Cargo and Trucks
- Search by route, type, or user
- Filter by status (All/Open/Waiting)
- Save and reuse search presets
- View active shipment cards

Truckers also get Route Optimizer access.

If your account is suspended, a red banner appears with a Pay Fees shortcut to contracts.

## 7. Shipper guide

### Post cargo
1. Tap `Post Cargo`.
2. Enter origin and destination.
3. Optionally add pickup and delivery street addresses.
4. Enter cargo type, weight, vehicle needed, budget, pickup date, and description.
5. Optionally upload photos.
6. Submit.

### Review bids
1. Open your cargo listing details.
2. Check incoming bids.
3. Accept or reject bids.
4. If needed, reopen listing for bidding.

### Book a truck listing
1. Open a truck listing.
2. Tap `Book Truck`.
3. Enter your offer, cargo type, cargo weight, and optional message.
4. Submit booking request.

### Contracts
- After a bid is accepted, a contract is created.
- If platform fee is not yet paid by trucker, shipper sees "awaiting payment" state.
- Once active, shipper can sign.
- After delivery, shipper can confirm delivery to complete the contract.

## 8. Trucker guide

### Post truck availability
1. Tap `Post Truck`.
2. Enter origin and destination.
3. Optionally add exact pickup/drop street addresses.
4. Enter vehicle type, rate, available date, and description.
5. Optionally upload photos.
6. Submit.

### Bid on cargo
1. Open a cargo listing.
2. Tap `Place Bid`.
3. Enter bid amount and optional message.
4. Submit bid.

### Pay platform fee (important)
- Platform fee is 5% of the contract amount.
- Trucker pays this fee via GCash before contract activation.
- Flow:
  1. Open contract payment prompt.
  2. Scan/pay using GCash QR or account details.
  3. Upload screenshot proof.
  4. Wait for verification status.
- Verification can be pending, manual review, approved, or rejected.

### Sign and fulfill contract
- After fee approval, sign the contract.
- Once both sides sign, shipment tracking becomes active.
- Update current location during transit from Tracking view.

## 9. Chat and privacy rules

- Chat is tied to a bid/booking context.
- Messages are sanitized to hide contact info, phone numbers, emails, and social links.
- Contact details are not exposed during negotiation.
- Contact details become visible in active/signed contract context.

## 10. Contracts and statuses

Common contract statuses:
- `pending_payment`: platform fee not yet paid/approved
- `draft`: ready for signatures
- `signed`: fully signed and active
- `in_transit`: shipment ongoing
- `completed`: delivery done
- `disputed`: under dispute handling
- `cancelled`: closed/cancelled

Contracts screen includes:
- Search
- Status filters
- Platform fee ledger summary
- Signature state (who has signed)

## 11. Tracking

Tracking screen shows:
- Active shipments
- Delivered shipments
- Progress bars
- Live location
- Tracking number

If you are the assigned trucker, you can update current location from city selector.

## 12. Notifications

Alerts screen supports:
- Mark single alert as read
- Mark all alerts as read
- Quick actions from alert cards:
  - Open Bid
  - Open Chat
  - Open Contract
  - Pay Now (for fee-related alerts)
  - Open Listing (for chat requests)

## 13. Broker program

### Join
- Open `Broker Program` and tap `Become a Broker`.

### What brokers can do
- Get a referral code and share link (`/r/CODE`).
- Track commission earnings.
- View payout history.
- Request payouts.

### Payout basics
- Minimum payout request: PHP 500.
- Methods include GCash and bank transfer.
- Payout requests require admin approval.

## 14. Admin guide

Admins can open `Admin Dashboard` and access:
- Dashboard overview
- Users
- Listings
- Contracts
- Shipments
- Payments
- Financial
- Disputes
- Referrals
- Broker payouts
- Ratings
- Settings

### Payment Review (important)
- Review flagged payment submissions.
- Inspect screenshot, OCR extraction, fraud score, and fraud flags.
- Approve or reject submissions with notes.

### Contract Verification
- Run verification checks to confirm approved platform-fee payments result in contract creation.
- Review success/failure counts and issue details.

## 15. Profile and account management

In Profile, you can:
- Edit personal and business details
- Switch role (Shipper/Trucker)
- Check stats, tier/badge, and wallet snapshot
- Sign out

## 16. Helpful tips

- Use exact city names for better route and tracking results.
- Add street-level address details to reduce pickup/dropoff confusion.
- Upload clear payment screenshots to avoid rejection.
- Use saved searches and saved routes for frequent operations.
- Check Alerts often for bid, contract, and payment updates.

## 17. Troubleshooting

### "I cannot bid or book"
Possible reasons:
- You are not signed in.
- Listing is not open.
- Your account is suspended due to unpaid platform fees.

### "My payment is still not approved"
- Status may be `pending` or `manual_review`.
- Ensure screenshot is clear and amount/receiver match the required values.
- If rejected, re-submit with a clearer and correct screenshot.

### "I cannot see contract actions"
- Actions depend on your role and current contract status.
- Some actions only appear after platform fee approval.

### "I cannot access admin dashboard"
- Only admin accounts can access admin features.
