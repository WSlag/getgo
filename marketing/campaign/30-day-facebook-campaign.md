# GetGo PH — 30-Day Facebook Marketing Campaign
**App:** GetGo PH (karga.ph / getgoph.web.app)
**Brand:** GetGo / Karga Connect
**Period:** Day 1–30 (launch anytime)
**Daily Budget:** ₱150/day → ₱4,500/month total
**Target Market:** Philippines nationwide — heavy Facebook users
**Audiences:** (A) Cargo Owners / Shippers | (B) Freight Brokers / Agents
**Objective:** Awareness → App Installs → Active Bookings

---

## SMART BUDGET STRATEGY (₱150/day)

With ₱150/day we run **one well-targeted ad set at a time** and rotate every 3–5 days.
Facebook's minimum effective budget per ad set is ₱100–₱150/day, so this is a single-focus strategy.

### Monthly Budget Breakdown

| Phase | Days | Daily | Total | Goal |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Phase 1 — Awareness | 1–10 | ₱150 | ₱1,500 | Reach, video views, brand recall |
| Phase 2 — Consideration | 11–20 | ₱150 | ₱1,500 | App installs, lead forms |
| Phase 3 — Conversion | 21–30 | ₱150 | ₱1,500 | First bookings, broker sign-ups |
| **TOTAL** | **30 days** | **₱150** | **₱4,500** | |

### Active Ad Set Rotation (One at a time)

| Days | Active Ad Set | Objective |
|---|---|---|
| 1–5 | Shippers — Nationwide — Video Views | Brand awareness |
| 6–10 | Brokers — Nationwide — Video Views | Broker awareness |

| 21–25 | Retargeting — Video Viewers | Warm audience conversion |
